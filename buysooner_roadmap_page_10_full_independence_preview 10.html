Your Path to Full Independence

Moving from the “Bridge” to 100% Ownership.

BuySooner is designed to be your head start, not a forever arrangement. Our goal is to see you transition into a standard bank mortgage as soon as the numbers align. When that happens, BuySooner steps out, and you move forward with 100% of the future growth in your St Leonards home.

The exit is not about guessing a date. It is about the strength of your position.

Three Scenarios for Your Exit

The Cautious Market

4.0% p.a. growth
Likely refinance Year 5

If the market takes a steadier breath, we simply stay on the bridge a little longer to help ensure your refinance position is secure.

A slower market may need more time before BuySooner can step out.

The Roadmap Base Case

5.0% p.a. growth
Likely refinance Year 4

Year 3 will be close, but Year 4 likely provides the cleanest pathway to transition to a standard bank loan.

This is the central Roadmap case used for the personalised illustration.

The Stronger Market

7.0% p.a. growth
Likely refinance Year 3

A stronger market accelerates your equity, potentially allowing BuySooner to step out as early as Year 3.

Faster growth may bring the refinance pathway forward.

The Bottom Line

The faster your equity grows and the more you pay down your mortgage, the sooner you reach full independence. We are here to help you monitor the market so you can make the move at the right time for you.
